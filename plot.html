PLOT

Here the film consists of a brief message which is to be aware of your water utilization. Running taps and everlasting showers are matters to be concerned about. It simply means making use of water in an appropriate and judicious manner. Since our lives depend entirely on water, it is our duty to be concerned about water conservation and how we can contribute to it. All in all, we must identify water scarcity as a real issue as it is very dangerous. Further, after identifying it, we must make sure to take steps to utilize and conserve it.
